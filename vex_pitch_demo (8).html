Awaab's Law Explained
October 2025 — Now in force
Your landlord must investigate within 14 days of being notified about damp or mould, and begin repairs within a further 7 days if a hazard is confirmed. Vex provides objective, time-stamped evidence to support your case.
One number, one sentence

Push notifications at the right time
Tenants are only alerted when the signal crosses a meaningful threshold — not every reading. Signal-to-noise ratio is everything.

The Rights tab tracks every step of the Awaab's Law process. One tap generates a timestamped PDF ready to send to the landlord.
